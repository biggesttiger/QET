Quantum Equation Technology (QET) Agent Policy

1. Overview

QET‚Äôs Agent Model is designed to incentivize sales representatives while ensuring a sustainable, non-Ponzi structure. Agents earn commissions on NFT sales and resales while also qualifying for monthly salaries based on performance and retention. This model prevents abuse by requiring ongoing sales and maintaining active holders.

2. Agent Tiers & Compensation Structure

Agents progress through different tiers based on performance, starting with commission-only earnings before unlocking monthly salaries at higher levels.

2.1 Commission Structure

  • Direct NFT Sale Commission: Agents earn 10% on every NFT sale. This is split into:
    • 5% Instant Payment (paid immediately upon sale)
    • 5% Spread Payment (paid monthly over 12 months)
  • NFT Resale Commission: Agents earn 1% of resale value whenever a holder resells an NFT on OpenSea or any other secondary market.

2.2 Agent Tiers & Salary Progression

Agents begin at the Starter Tier, earning commissions only. They qualify for salaries after meeting specific milestones.

3. Qualification & Performance Requirements

To prevent abuse, agents must meet the following requirements to maintain their salary:

3.1 Ongoing Sales Requirement

  • Agents must generate new NFT sales at 20% of their tier level every 3 months to continue receiving salaries.
  • If an agent fails to meet their quota, their salary is paused until they reach the quota again.

3.2 Rolling Active Holder Requirement

  • At least 75% of required active holders must remain every 3 months to avoid a salary freeze.
  • If an agent‚Äôs holder retention drops below 75%, their salary is reduced or paused.

5. Anti-Gaming & Fraud Prevention

  • Resale Disqualification: If an NFT is resold, the original agent does not receive credit for the new holder. Only the first sale counts towards their qualification.
  • No Self-Referrals: Agents cannot purchase NFTs under their own referral to qualify for salaries.
  • Blacklist Policy: Agents found exploiting loopholes (e.g., fake accounts, artificial sales) will be permanently banned.
  • KYC Verification: Every agent must pass a Know Your Customer (KYC) check to qualify.

6. Payment & Payout System

  • Commissions and salaries are paid 70% in USDT and 30% in QET tokens.
  • Payouts are processed monthly, with spread commissions released in equal parts over 12 months.
  • If an agent misses two consecutive sales quotas, salary payments are halted until requirements are met again.
