Quantum Equation Technology (QET) Whitepaper

Abstract

Quantum Equation Technology (QET) introduces a revolutionary investment model that merges blockchain-powered NFTs with sustainable real-world investments. Investors can purchase smart contract-backed NFTs that yield a 3% monthly return on investment (ROI), paid in 70% USDT and 30% QET tokens. These NFTs are fully transferable and tradable on open marketplaces such as OpenSea and Blur, allowing holders to liquidate their investments seamlessly. The project is supported by a liquidity pool backed by locked funds and real-world investments, ensuring long-term stability and sustainable returns.

Introduction

The advent of decentralized finance (DeFi) and non-fungible tokens (NFTs) has opened up new opportunities for investors. QET leverages these technologies to offer a secure, automated, and transparent investment model. This whitepaper outlines the structure, tokenomics, governance, and security measures underpinning QET, as well as the mechanisms ensuring sustainable returns for NFT holders.

Features and Benefits

  • Smart Contract-Backed NFTs: Each NFT represents a stake in the QET ecosystem and entitles its holder to monthly ROI.
  • Sustainable ROI: Investors receive 3% monthly returns, distributed in 70% USDT and 30% QET.
  • Tradability: NFTs can be resold on open markets, allowing liquidity without traditional lock-in periods.
  • Liquidity Pool Backed by Real-World Investments: Ensures sustainable payouts and financial stability.
  • Deflationary Token Model: QET incorporates an auto-burn mechanism to reduce supply over time.
  • Community Governance: NFT holders can vote on key decisions, including minting, burning, and liquidity management.
  • Anti-Bot & Anti-Snipe Measures: Protects against automated trading exploits.
